In collaboration with the School of Education and Information Services at the University of Glasgow, I worked to improve the user experience of students undertaking fully online courses. I did this through a combination of A/B testing, eye-tracking data, and interviews. I then produced a report with recommendations for improvements which were implemented for the 2020 academic year and online teaching pivot.
